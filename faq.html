Frequently asked questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
Note: PayPal account required!
1. Go to "hz.mynetpay.nl/Login" and login with your MyHZ credentials.
2. Click on "Top-up balance" in the navigation bar to buy credit.
3. Pick an amount (I would suggest picking €5 if it's your first time; you can always buy more later).
4. Proceed to checkout and sign in using a PayPal account to complete your payment.
5. Once completed, you can click on "Web Print" in the navigation bar, then follow the instructions.
6. When going to the chosen printer, bring your student card for authorization!

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
1. At your chosen printer, show your student card.
2. Select "Scan" on the screen, then follow the instructions.
3. The scanned document will be sent to your HZ email.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
1. Go to "webshop.hz.nl".
2. Choose an item, then click "ORDER", "Add to shoppingcart".
3. Pay using PayPal or iDEAL.

How can you book a project space?
1. Go to Learn.hz.
2. On the navigation bar, click on "FACILITIES", "Selfservice portal", "Reserve".
3. Click on the tile saying "Reserve a conference or project room", then follow the instructions.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
1. Park your car at "parkeergarage Kousteensedijk", a parking garage located close to the library in Middelburg.
2. Before 4 PM, ask for a free parking ticket at the HZ front office.
3. When driving out of the parking garage, use both the ticket you got when entering the parking garage, and the free parking ticket.
